🅿 ParkAlert – Never get a parking ticket at the supermarket again!

You know how it goes: you pop in for a quick shop at the supermarket and come back to find a parking fine under your windscreen wiper. ParkAlert reminds you before your parking time runs out.

──────────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
──────────────────────────────
1. Open the app
2. Select your parking duration: 15, 30, 60, 90 or 120 minutes
3. Go shopping – ParkAlert runs in the background
4. At 10 minutes remaining: warning alert!
5. At 0 minutes: alarm + vibration

──────────────────────────────
FEATURES
──────────────────────────────
✅ 5 quick-select buttons: 15 / 30 / 60 / 90 / 120 minutes
✅ Large countdown display – easy to read even in bright sunlight
✅ Colour feedback: Green → Yellow (10 min) → Red (expired)
✅ Keeps running with screen locked (Foreground Service)
✅ Alarm notification + vibration when time expires
✅ Overtime display: shows how long you've been over the limit
✅ Android Auto support
✅ Free – ad-supported with GDPR-compliant consent
✅ No account, no registration, no cloud

──────────────────────────────
PERFECT FOR
──────────────────────────────
• Supermarket car parks and retail parking lots
• Time-limited parking zones
• Short-stay city centre parking
• Anywhere with a maximum parking duration
• Shopping centres and retail parks

──────────────────────────────
PRIVACY
──────────────────────────────
The app displays advertising via Google AdMob. On first launch you will be asked for your consent (GDPR / IAB TCF 2.2). You can say no at any time – the app works fully without consent. Your consent can be changed at any time in Settings.

No location data collected. No usage tracking by the app developer.
